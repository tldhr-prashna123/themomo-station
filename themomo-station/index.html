About Us

Your Favorite Local Momo Spot

Founded in 2015, The Momo Station has been serving the Sifal Ground area with delicious, authentic momos made with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes.

What started as a small street food stall has now become one of the most popular momo destinations in the city, known for our lightning-fast service and consistent quality.

We take pride in our secret spice blends and homemade sauces that make our momos truly special. Every bite is packed with flavor that keeps our customers coming back for more.
